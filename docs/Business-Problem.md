# Business Problem

## Background

Many organizations manage company assets using spreadsheets or outdated software. This makes it difficult to track asset ownership, maintenance history, depreciation, warranties, and inventory accurately.

As organizations grow, manual processes result in:

- Lost or misplaced assets
- Duplicate records
- Poor visibility of asset status
- Delayed maintenance
- Inaccurate reporting
- Security and compliance challenges

## Objective

Develop a modern Enterprise Asset Management System that enables organizations to efficiently manage physical and digital assets through a centralized, secure, and scalable platform.

The application should improve operational efficiency, provide real-time visibility into assets, and support future AI-powered capabilities.

## Expected Benefits

- Centralized asset management
- Improved operational efficiency
- Better reporting and analytics
- Secure role-based access
- Reduced manual work
- Scalable architecture
- AI-ready foundation
