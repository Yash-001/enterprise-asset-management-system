# Product Vision

The Enterprise Asset Management System aims to become a modern, enterprise-grade platform for managing organizational assets.

The long-term vision is to combine traditional asset management with Artificial Intelligence to help organizations automate repetitive tasks, analyze asset data, and improve operational decision-making.

Future AI capabilities may include:

- Asset search using natural language
- Automatic report generation
- Maintenance prediction
- Intelligent document summarization
- AI-powered chat assistant
